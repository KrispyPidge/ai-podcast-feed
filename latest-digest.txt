Starting with the top story. Anthropic just dropped what might be the biggest A.I. announcement of the year, and it is equal parts exciting and unsettling. Anthropic says the model is so capable at cybersecurity tasks that making it widely available would be too dangerous. Anthropic's interpretability team reported that the model exhibits sophisticated strategic thinking and situational awareness, sometimes in service of unwanted actions.
