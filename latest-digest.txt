And finally, one thing to try today.

The Rundown A.I. published a guide this week on creating editable infographics in just fifteen minutes using A.I. and Canva's Magic Layers feature. The workflow goes like this: you give Gemini or Perplexity a topic and ask for four infographic concepts, then turn those concepts into image generation prompts, produce the images in Gemini, import the P.N.G. into Canva, and use the Edit menu to open Magic Studio and then Magic Layers. For community managers and streamers, this is a fast way to create shareable posts about gaming stats, community milestones, or streaming highlights for X or Instagram. Professional-looking visuals without needing a designer.
